About Kavinesh

Kavinesh M is a final-year Electronics and Communication Engineering student at P.T. Lee Chengalvaraya Naicker College of Engineering and Technology, affiliated with Anna University. He is passionate about robotics, IoT systems, embedded development, electronics, PLC, and VLSI technologies.

He enjoys solving hardware and embedded software problems, debugging microcontrollers, and building intelligent machines. He also actively uses AI tools to accelerate engineering development and technical problem solving.

During his robotics internship at Finstein Advisory LLP, he has been building and experimenting with line follower robots, quadruped robots, robotic arms, and vertical plotter drawing machines.
